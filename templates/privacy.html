Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps, used only for security and debugging.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and summaries.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve the Service.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers located in India. We take reasonable technical measures — including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls — to protect your data from unauthorised access.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all associated personal data and expense records are permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts: fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log your IP address as part of this request. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via the support link on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy-related queries within 5 business days.
